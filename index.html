Darci Sutton

I've never been a huge fan of the saying "jack of all trades, master of none." It's always been the "master of NONE" part that got me. It's really given us multitaskers a complex…I much prefer "I wear a lot of hats."

I dabble in all things creative – I love production, post-production, creative concepting, editing, designing, storyboarding, marketing, and branding. While I do enjoy partaking in all of the previously mentioned creative activities, I am also fantastic at organizing them and keeping the artists who do them on track to meet deadlines. That way, brilliance can come together to create a product instead of wandering aimlessly with no clear path. Essentially, I'm a producer…a producer who really cares about the creative. Yes, I'm known for being the one who can handle logistics, but all of my logistical sense comes together because of my ability to conceive and execute a creative vision.
